The Angel with no Wings v. 1.0
A map by Dragonfly, Pekka Lampinen (seppo.lampinen@kolumbus.fi)
Medium sized map w/ underground
1 human player (red) & 3 computer players (blue, tan, green)
Special victory condition: transport a specific artifact
Special loss condition: run out of time
Difficulty level: Normal
Scenario description: The person whose guardian angel you were has died. You tried to save him from the minotaur, but you lost your wings helping him. If you cannot find them in three months, you cannot return to Heaven. If the forces of Angel fall, your quest no longer has a purpose.
Other playtesters: Lauri Palasto
