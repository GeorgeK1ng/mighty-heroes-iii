Winter Palace

By Scrowe
Simon_crowe@excite.com


Size: Small
Players: 1 human, 1 computer
Difficulty: Normal
Victory Conditions: Capture the Ice Palace 

Description:
The Ice Palace is an evil place, no matter who resides there they will become corrupted.
The once kindly Ice Queen made it her home, and now she has brought evil back to the lands.
You must capture the Ice Palace to stop this from happening again!
