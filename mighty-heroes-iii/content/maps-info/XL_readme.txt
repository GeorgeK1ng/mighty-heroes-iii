			Heroes 3 Map



Map Name: 	The Ways  
Author:    	Peter Nap (peternap@dds.nl)         
Requirements:   Heroes3 v1.1
Map size:       Extra Large   (with underground Maze)
Single Player:  Yes
Multiplayer:    Yes 
Max.Players:	6 (and 2 AI Players)



Storyline:
Based on the novel of Robert Jordan, this map creates an atmosphere of the great writer and his famous WAYS. Break the seals of the Dark One's Prison yourself and defeat the Father of Lies. To break the seals, you must 'unite' all the countries.
